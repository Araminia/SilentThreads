Hello 💜

Hello, loveee. I’m super tired emotionally right now. Parang ang bigat-bigat lang ng nararamdaman ko today, and I just really want to rest kahit sandali lang. I want to rant sa’yo sana, to tell you everything that’s been on my mind and in my heart. I need you right now, mahaal.

Pero at the same time, I know you’re tired too. Alam ko rin na marami kang ginagawa at pagod ka rin sa sarili mong mga responsibilities. That’s why sometimes I stop myself from saying too much, kasi ayokong dumagdag pa sa pagod at pressure na nararamdaman mo. Ayokong maramdaman mo na kailangan mo pang i-handle lahat ng emotions ko when you’re already exhausted. Naiisip ko rin na baka lalo ka lang mapagod dahil sa’kin.

Pero mahal, I just want to be honest with you. I really miss you so much. I miss talking to you the way we used to. I miss those moments na kahit simpleng kwentuhan lang tayo, or kahit random na usapan lang natin, it already makes my day feel lighter. I miss your presence, your attention, and the way you make me feel safe and calm. I miss everything about you, mahaaal ko.

Sometimes I tell you how much I miss you because it’s really what I feel. Gusto ko lang iparamdam sa’yo kung gaano kita ka-miss at kung gaano ka ka-importante sa’kin. But sometimes when I say those things, parang hindi mo napapansin or hindi mo masyadong nirereactan. And honestly, it hurts me sometimes. Not because I’m demanding anything from you, but because those words come from a very sincere place in my heart.

Pero kahit ganon, I still try to understand you. I tell myself na baka busy ka lang, or maybe pagod ka lang talaga at wala ka lang energy to respond the way you normally would. Iniintindi ko na lang, kahit minsan napapangiti na lang ako and say “okay lang, baka busy lang siya.” Hehe.

I just want you to know that even if I feel this way sometimes, I’m still here. I still care about you the same way. I still miss you the same way. And I still love you just as much as before. I don’t want to make you feel pressured or guilty. Gusto ko lang maging honest sa nararamdaman ko because you’re the person I want to be vulnerable with.

Right now, I just really wish I could rest my heart for a bit with you. Kahit saglit lang na maramdaman ko na okay lang mapagod, okay lang maging emotional, because you’re there. I miss you so much, mahaaal ko. I really do.

I love you so much, lovee! And I really miss you.
