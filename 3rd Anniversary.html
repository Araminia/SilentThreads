Happy 3rd Anniversary, My Love! 💜

Happy anniversary. As I sit down to write this, I can’t help but look back on everything we’ve been through, every day of waiting, every night of missing each other, and every moment that tested our love. Kahit gaano pa kalayo ang pagitan natin at kahit gaano man kabagal lumipas ang oras, ikaw pa rin ang laman ng puso at dasal ko.

There were nights filled with tears and days heavy with longing, but in every pain and every challenge, I always chose you. I still choose you. Because no matter how difficult the distance or how long the wait, my heart has never wavered. Nandito pa rin ako, hindi bibitaw, at ikaw pa rin ang uuwian ko.

When the world feels confusing and the future seems uncertain, one thing remains clear to me, you are my pahinga, my comfort, and my greatest blessing. Sa lahat ng pagsubok na dumaan at darating pa, alam kong kakayanin natin, as long as we stay tapat at totoo sa isa’t isa. Our love has endured not because it is easy, but because it is real and worth fighting for.

Thank you for loving me, for staying, and for choosing us every single day. On this anniversary, I just want you to know that my heart is still yours—steady, certain, and full of hope for the future we are building together.

No matter the distance, no matter the time, I will remain yours. I love you, not just for now but for every forever waiting in every universe.
